Our Shop

Come and enjoy a "back-in-the-day" atmosphere with some of the finest stylists. You will be pleased by the professionalism displayed from the staff and comfortable waiting area. As founder and owner (Gary Martinez, licensed Master Barber) of Break Through Styles Barbershop, it is my duty to ensure we exhibit professional customer service to our clients.
